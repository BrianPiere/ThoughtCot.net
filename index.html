Revenue Model

Submissions of every kind (i.e. beliefs, questions, and contradictions) will require “gas fees” to run on the Ethereum network. Users will have a “slider” where they may spend more tokens in order to entice miners to add records onto the blockchain sooner (being first has implications).

Belief or question “providers” may amplify the visibility with tokens. If a statement is fought to be 70% right on average, only 30% of the bounty is owed.

Revenue is generated by:

  • Collecting exactly 50% of a users choice to spend gas per entry. (ETH * 2).
  • Skimming 2% of any coins paid out as a bounty (we only skim movement).
