Which Problems Do We Solve?

Problems

  • No mechanism to determine who's won an argument without involving votes/opinions and/or judges/authority.
  • With the promise of trust that blockchain offers, there's never been an effort to decentralize the truth.
  • The same talking points are repeated hourly on the nightly news and rehashed countless times in forums without changing minds.
  • There's no economy allowing those with a complicated theses or bold ideas to attract detailed investigations from professionals.

Solutions

  • The last word wins in a game of attrition, supported by a protocol to keep people focused on the mistakes.
  • Let everything enjoy truth by default... claims, contradictions, and all else. What is right is what stands free of contradictions among the collective.
  • Recycle contradictions for all time whenever similar context is encountered. The negative portion of a contradiction (i.e. "the can't") serves as an indexing mechanism.
  • Allow anyone to make bold claims, and back them up, with money on the line.
Build up your "Trust Factor" by winning arguments. A contradiction process forces people to focus on mistakes, not rights.
